# Discipline-Duel
Slay the excuses holding you back with The Discipline Duel! This interactive game from TLFY challenges your mindset by pitting common hurdles against powerful "Counter-Strikes." Build your Resilience HP and boost your Future Focus Score as you master the discipline needed for life and tech.
