# Discipline-Duel
Slay the excuses holding you back with The Discipline Duel! This interactive game from TLFY challenges your mindset by pitting common hurdles against powerful "Counter-Strikes." Build your Resilience HP and boost your Future Focus Score as you master the discipline needed for life and tech. Face the duel, drop the excuses, and level up! ![The Discipline Duel](discipline-duel-game-art.jpg)
